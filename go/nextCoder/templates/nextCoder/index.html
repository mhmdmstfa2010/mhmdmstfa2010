WHAT IS NEXT CODER

Next coder is a platform in which people can learn to code and help others to do so. It’s a collaborative, non-profit app in which you can attend (or hold) presentations about different tech-related content as well as private free tutoring.

OUR MISSION

Our goal is to provide access to a high-quality education to everyone. Unfortunately, most people will never try out programming as it’s not included in most educating systems. Therefore, next coder try to keep it easy to all of the people who are willing to give it a try. We tried to make a collaborative environment to learn, teach and have fun all at the same time.

WHY NEXT CODER

Learning to code can be overwhelming at first, thus we have created a platform in which you can always be helped by industry-professionals to whom you can ask anything. Moreover, you should make sure to help others as well to keep next coder a cooperative environment.
